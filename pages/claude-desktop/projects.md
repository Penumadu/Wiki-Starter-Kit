# Projects: Your Custom Knowledge Base

**Summary:** Projects allow you to ground Claude in your specific data—PDFs, codebases, and meeting notes—creating a "mini-AI" that knows everything about your current work.

**The "Non-Tech" Translation:**
Think of a Project like giving Claude a "Specialized Backpack." Inside that backpack, you put all the books and notes it needs for a specific class. When you're in that Project, Claude only looks inside that backpack to answer your questions.

### Steps to Setup Your First Project

1. **Create New Project:** Click "Projects" in the left sidebar and then "+ New Project."
2. **Add Knowledge:** Upload your files (up to 200k tokens per project). This could be your company's HR handbook, a 100-page research paper, or a ZIP of your code.
3. **Set Project Instructions:** Tell Claude *how* to behave in this project. E.g., "Always respond as a Technical Lead for this specific codebase."
4. **Chat & Iterate:** Start a new chat within the Project. Claude will remember all the files you uploaded every time you start a new thread.

**The "Aha!" Moment:**
Uploading a 300-page technical manual and then asking, "According to chapter 4, what is the torque specification for the main bolt?" and getting an answer in 3 seconds.

**Pro-Tip:**
Use **Claude Cowork** to share projects with your team. Anyone in the Cowork space can add files to the project, making it a "Living Brain" for your department.
