# Workflows: Pre-Built Automation Paths

**Summary:** Workflows are structured sequences of prompts and tool calls that automate repetitive tasks, like "summarize my emails every morning" or "audit my code on every push."

**The "Non-Tech" Translation:**
Think of a Workflow like a **"Recipe Card."** Instead of telling Claude every single step every time, you just hand it the recipe card for "Morning Coffee," and it knows exactly which beans to grind, how much water to use, and when to pour.

### Steps to Use Workflows

1. **Browse the Library:** Look for the "Workflows" tab in Claude Desktop.
2. **Select a Template:** Choose a pre-built workflow (e.g., "Daily Social Media Post" or "Legal Document Review").
3. **Configure Inputs:** Tell the workflow where to look. E.g., "Use my 'Company Projects' folder as the source."
4. **Run & Schedule:** Execute the workflow manually or schedule it to run automatically at a specific time.

**The "Aha!" Moment:**
Setting up a "Content Repurposing" workflow that takes a YouTube transcript and automatically generates 5 Tweets, 1 LinkedIn post, and a Newsletter draft in your specific voice.

**Pro-Tip:**
Combine **Workflows** with **MCP** to create "Auto-Agents" that can fetch data from a database, analyze it, and email a report to your manager without you touching a single button.
