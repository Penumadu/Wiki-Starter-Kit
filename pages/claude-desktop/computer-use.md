# Computer Use: Hands-Free Productivity

**Summary:** Computer Use allows Claude to actually take control of your mouse and keyboard to complete complex, multi-step tasks across different applications.

**The "Non-Tech" Translation:**
Imagine you hired an intern, gave them your mouse, and said, "Go open my email, find the invoice from Apple, download it, and put it in my Accounting folder." Computer Use is exactly that—an intern moving your mouse for you.

### ⚠️ Safety Warning
> [!WARNING]
> **Computer Use requires extreme care.** Because the AI is actually moving your mouse and clicking buttons, you must grant it explicit operating system permissions. Never leave the AI unattended while it is controlling your computer, and ensure you do not have sensitive information open that you don't want it to see.

### Context-Aware Recommendations
Perplexity's new "Personal Computer" update is great for *cloud-based* orchestration, but Claude's Computer Use (via the Anthropic API or Claude Code) is unparalleled for raw, visual interaction with *local* desktop applications that don't have APIs.

### Steps to Enable Computer Use

1. **Understand the Tool:** In 2026, this feature is primarily accessed via Claude Code or specific Desktop integrations.
2. **Grant OS Permissions:** Your Mac or Windows machine will prompt you to allow "Screen Recording" and "Accessibility Control." You must approve these for the feature to work.
3. **Give a Clear Command:** Be highly specific. E.g., "Open Safari, go to weather.com, check the forecast for Toronto, and create a new note in Apple Notes with the result."
4. **Supervise:** Watch the screen as Claude moves the cursor and types. You can usually press `Escape` or a dedicated hotkey to interrupt it if it makes a mistake.

**The "Aha!" Moment:**
Watching the cursor move entirely on its own, open a spreadsheet, copy a specific cell, open an email draft, paste the number, and hit send.

**Next Action:**
Review your OS privacy settings to understand exactly which apps currently have "Accessibility" access to control your computer.
