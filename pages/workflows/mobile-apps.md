# Mobile Workflows (iOS & Android)

While the Desktop apps are the command centers for heavy lifting, the Claude and Perplexity mobile apps are essential tools for "on-the-go" workflows, leveraging your phone's unique hardware: the camera, the microphone, and location services.

## Perplexity Mobile: Voice Search & Location

The Perplexity app is designed to replace your default mobile search engine (like Safari or Chrome).

### Advanced Voice Mode
Instead of typing, you can tap the microphone and ask a long, rambling question. Perplexity will transcribe it, clean it up, and run a Pro Search. 
*Example:* While driving, "Hey Perplexity, I'm driving through the Finger Lakes region in New York right now. What are three highly-rated wineries near me that have outdoor seating and serve food? Give me their addresses."

### Location Awareness
Because the mobile app has GPS access (if enabled), you don't need to specify where you are. 
*Example:* "Where is the nearest hardware store that has a 5/8 inch drill bit currently in stock?" The app will cross-reference your GPS with live local inventory data.

## Claude Mobile: Vision & Voice Chat

The Claude mobile app excels at interacting with the physical world around you.

### Real-Time Vision
You can take a photo natively in the Claude app and ask it to analyze the scene immediately.
* **The Menu Translation:** Take a photo of a menu in a foreign language. "Translate this, but also tell me which dish is the most traditional for this region."
* **The IT Fix:** Take a photo of the error screen on your laptop. "Why is my computer showing a Blue Screen of Death with error code 0x0000003B?"

### Conversational Voice Mode
Unlike standard dictation, Claude's mobile app features a true conversational voice mode. You can put your phone on the table and have a fluid, back-and-forth verbal brainstorming session. Claude will listen, wait for you to finish, and speak back with a natural-sounding voice.

## The "Mobile to Desktop" Handoff

The most powerful way to use the mobile apps is in tandem with your desktop. Both apps sync your history instantly.

**The Workflow:**
1. **Capture (Mobile):** You are at a conference. You take a photo of a speaker's complex presentation slide using the Claude mobile app.
2. **Draft (Mobile):** You use Voice Mode to say, *"Extract the data from this slide into a table."*
3. **Execute (Desktop):** You get back to your hotel, open Claude Desktop, open the same chat thread, and say: *"Take the table we made earlier and use Claude Code to turn it into a React dashboard component."*

This seamless handoff allows you to capture raw data in the real world and process it later with the full power of the desktop environment.
