# Buy with Pro (Shopping Integration)

Perplexity isn't just for researching historical facts or writing code; it is a powerful e-commerce engine. With the **Buy with Pro** integration, Perplexity becomes your personal AI shopping assistant.

## What is Buy with Pro?

Instead of spending hours reading reviews on Amazon, watching YouTube videos, and comparing prices across different tabs, Perplexity synthesizes all product data into one view. 

If you are a Perplexity Pro subscriber, you can securely store your shipping and payment information. When you find the product you want, you can click "Buy with Pro," and Perplexity will automatically purchase the item for you directly from the merchant, handling the checkout process in the background.

## The Shopping Workflow

1. **The Research Phase:**
Ask a complex shopping query.

```text
[TRY IT]
I need a standing desk for my home office. It must be motorized, have a real wood top (not laminate), and cost under $600. Find the top 3 options based on Reddit reviews and compare their warranty lengths.
```

2. **The Product Cards:**
Perplexity will return detailed product cards. These cards include:
- A synthesized consensus of user reviews (both positive and negative).
- Real-time pricing from multiple vendors.
- A "Pros and Cons" list extracted from expert blogs.

3. **The Purchase:**
If a product is eligible for "Buy with Pro," a button will appear. Click it, and the item will be ordered without you ever having to leave the Perplexity interface or create an account on the merchant's site.

## Tracking & Returns

- **Centralized Tracking:** All orders placed through Perplexity are tracked within the app. You can see shipping status directly in your Pro dashboard.
- **Customer Support:** If you need to return an item, Perplexity assists with the return routing, acting as the intermediary between you and the original seller.

## Pro Tips

> **Pro-Tip:** Use Perplexity for "Compatibility Checks." If you are building a PC or buying car parts, ask: *"Is the AMD Ryzen 7 7800X3D compatible with the MSI MAG B650 Tomahawk motherboard? Are there any BIOS update requirements?"* Perplexity will search the exact specs and confirm compatibility.

> **Warning:** Buy with Pro is currently only available for shipping addresses within the United States. If you are international, you will still see the product research, but you will have to click the external link to purchase.
