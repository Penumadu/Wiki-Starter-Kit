# The Discover Feed

While you normally go to a search engine with a specific question, the **Perplexity Discover Feed** turns the platform into a personalized, AI-curated news aggregator.

## What is the Discover Feed?

Located on the homepage of the desktop and mobile apps, the Discover Feed is a constantly updating stream of deep-dive articles generated by Perplexity based on real-time trending topics and your personal interests.

> **Expert Tip:** Think of the Discover Feed as a personalized Sunday newspaper, where every article has been heavily researched, summarized, and fact-checked by AI specifically for you.

## How It Works

1. **Global Trends:** Perplexity constantly monitors the internet for breaking news, scientific breakthroughs, and cultural trends.
2. **AI Generation:** When a topic trends, Perplexity's AI automatically researches it, creates a beautifully formatted Page with images and citations, and pushes it to the Discover Feed.
3. **Personalization:** Based on your AI Profile and past search history, Perplexity curates the feed so you see topics relevant to your industry.

## Interacting with the Feed

The Discover Feed isn't just static news. It's interactive.

- **Ask Follow-Ups:** If you read a Discover article about a new AI model release, you can click "Ask Follow Up" at the bottom to transition the article directly into a chat thread.
- **Save to Collections:** You can bookmark interesting Discover articles into your Custom Collections for later reference.

## Daily Digests

For Pro users, Perplexity can generate a **Daily Digest**—a custom audio or text summary of the top 5 things you need to know today in your specific industry. 

```text
[TRY IT]
Go to your Perplexity Settings -> AI Profile. Add: "I am a Supply Chain Manager specializing in semiconductor manufacturing. Every day, I need to know about geopolitical events affecting Taiwan, shipping lane disruptions, and raw material pricing."
```

Once configured, your Discover Feed will automatically surface the exact intelligence you need to do your job, synthesized from thousands of global news sources.
