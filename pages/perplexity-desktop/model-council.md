# Model Council: Parallel Power

**Summary:** The Model Council is a specialized feature that runs your query across three different frontier models (e.g., GPT-5, Gemini 2.0, and Claude 4.7) in parallel to find the most accurate and nuanced answer.

**The "Non-Tech" Translation:**
Think of the Model Council like a **"Supreme Court."** Instead of asking one judge for their opinion, you ask three world-class experts at the same time. If all three agree, you know the answer is solid. If they disagree, you can see exactly where the debate is.

### Steps to Use the Model Council

1. **Activate Council Mode:** Select "Model Council" from the dropdown menu (available to Max subscribers).
2. **Input Your Query:** Ask something complex or controversial. E.g., "Analyze the long-term impact of the 2026 AI Regulation Act on small startups."
3. **Compare Perspectives:** Perplexity will show you a "Consensus" view, followed by specific insights from each model (e.g., "Claude's View," "Gemini's View").
4. **Identify Hallucinations:** If one model makes a claim that the other two refute, Perplexity will flag it as a potential discrepancy.

**The "Aha!" Moment:**
Asking for a complex code refactor and seeing Claude's logic-first approach compared to GPT's efficiency-first approach, allowing you to choose the best parts of both.

**Pro-Tip:**
Use Model Council when you are fact-checking something highly technical. The diversity of training data across the three models makes it much harder for a single error to slip through.
