The "Getting Started" Path

Claude: First 30 Mins

  1. Enable Artifacts: Go to settings and ensure "Artifacts" is on to see code and docs side-by-side.
  2. The Deep Context Dump: Upload a 50-page PDF or a codebase and ask "Summarize the architectural bottlenecks."
  3. The Refinement Loop: Ask Claude to write a draft, then give it "vibes" feedback (e.g., "Make it more punchy").

Perplexity: First 30 Mins

  1. Set the Focus: Use the "Focus" button to toggle between Web, Academic, or Social (Reddit).
  2. The Rabbit Hole: Ask a broad question, then use the "Related" suggestions to dive deeper.
  3. Verify Everything: Click the small numbers [1], [2] to jump directly to the source article.

Power User Workflow: The AI Sandwich

The "AI Sandwich" is a workflow where you use Perplexity to gather raw ingredients, Claude to cook the meal, and Perplexity again to plate it with citations.

Prompt Library: Golden Templates

Deep Research (Perplexity)

Find the latest [Topic] trends for 2026. Focus on [Specific Niche]. Provide a list of the top 5 sources, summarize their main arguments, and identify any conflicting viewpoints between them.

Code Debugging (Claude)

I am getting the following error: [Error Message]. Here is my code: [Code]. Analyze the logic, identify the root cause, and provide a refactored version that follows [Best Practice/Style Guide]. Explain why your solution is better.

Creative Drafting (Claude)

Act as a world-class copywriter. Write a [Type of Content] for [Target Audience] about [Subject]. Use a [Tone] voice. Include a compelling hook, 3 value-driven sections, and a clear call to action. Avoid clichés.

Feature Spotlight

Claude: Artifacts & Projects

Artifacts: A dedicated window for code, diagrams, and websites. It lets you iterate visually without re-reading the whole chat.

Projects: Upload your entire library of docs/code once. Claude will remember them across every conversation in that project.

Perplexity: Collections & Pages

Collections: Folders for your threads. You can set a "Custom Instruction" for each collection (e.g., "Always cite academic papers").

Pages: Convert a search thread into a beautiful, shareable report with headers, images, and embedded sources with one click.
